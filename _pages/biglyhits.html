Bigly Hits

President Trump's music career has spawned some gems, and the only logical thing to do is release a compilation album filled with said gems. All tracks have been slightly remixed for that perfect sound we didn't get the first time around. And you can't do a compilation album without some goodies! The last two tracks are an early version of The Liberals, and a mashup of outtakes from various recording sessions.
